Website Evaluating for SpaceX

Site Info:

  • URL of the site:

    https://www.spacex.com/

  • Name of the site:

    SpaceX

  • The targeted audience:

    Nerds, people who want to be astronauts, people who would like to invest in the company's growth, and people who just want to watch the company grow.

Site Organization:

The site is organized using the Linear Organization method.

Site C.R.A.P Design:

The site is using a contrast design. one example is the sites white logo against the dark background.

Site Audit Score:

The site audit score is 85.

Site Effectiveness:

The site allows users to explore the specs of the rocket engines, watch live streams of onging mission, and read information about past or present or future missions.

Site Efficiency:

The site allows users to reach any information fast using ther navigation tab at the top of the site.

Site Engagement:

The site welcome users with videos about completed mission, upcoming launch, and recent launch as they scroll down.

My recommended improvement for the site:

The site already has a 3D model of the startship rocket. What I think they should do is Make it intrective with the user so they can move it around and view other angle of the rocket.
